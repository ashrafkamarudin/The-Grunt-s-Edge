# Arc Conflicts

*Note: Eric's specific progression within these arcs is not yet decided.*

---

## Activation Event

The ancient elven ruins in the north-east forest have been uncovered. A burst of magical energy erupted from within, drawing monsters from across the region. The monsters are moving toward the ruins — and scattering toward the Empire's north-east garrisons in the process.

Extra conscription is called to reinforce the north-east fort.

Eric is part of this wave.

---

## 2nd Arc — Fort Life & Monster Patrols

**Core situation:** Eric arrives at the north-east garrison fort. The fort is under pressure from monster activity driven by the ruins. Patrols, skirmishes, and defense duty.

**The ruins:** A known objective, but unreachable. The forest between the fort and the ruins is dense monster territory. No direct conflict with the Sylvan Reach yet — the monsters are the immediate enemy.

**Key tensions:**
- Fort life: hierarchy, supply shortages, the grind of garrison duty
- Patrols into monster territory — small skirmishes, not full battle
- Rumors about what's in the ruins spread through camp talk and officer leaks
- Occasional elite units pass through the fort on their way deeper into the forest — Eric sees them but doesn't join them
- The Sylvan Reach is watching. Elven scouts may be spotted near the forest edge. No open conflict, but tension builds.

---

## 3rd Arc — The Push

**Core situation:** The Empire mobilizes for a larger operation into the forest. The ruins become an active objective. Tensions with the Sylvan Reach escalate.

**Key tensions:**
- The contest for the ruins moves from background to foreground
- The Sylvan Reach may attempt their own recovery or contest the Empire's advance
- Monster resistance intensifies as forces push deeper into mana-rich territory
- Eric may be involved in supporting operations — supply lines, secondary positions, rear-guard actions — not the main push
- The political layer becomes visible: noble interests, sponsored heroes, propaganda about the ruins
- The gap between official accounts and what soldiers on the ground see grows wider

---

## Story Threads

### The Ruins
- Ancient elven technology and crafts
- Lost knowledge — things even the Sylvan Reach no longer possesses
- Riches that could shift the balance of power
- Both the Empire and the Sylvan Reach want it
- The forest belongs to the monsters — neither side can take it easily

### Monsters
- Drawn by the mana burst from the ruins
- Growing stronger near the source — denser mana produces stronger monsters
- Not mindless — territorial, hierarchical, some consume others for mana
- Fighting the Empire's forces and each other
- The deeper you go into the forest, the worse they get

### The Sylvan Reach
- Not at war with the Empire
- Their heritage sits in monster territory
- They may send scouts, agents, or small teams
- Open conflict with the Empire is possible if the ruins are breached
- For now, watching and waiting

### Noble Politics (Internal)
- The Crown needs the ruins' resources
- Nobles see opportunity — sponsoring heroes, positioning their houses
- Some nobles may be reluctant to commit troops
- Propaganda shapes how the ruins are framed to the public
- The fort's supply and reinforcement depends on political will
