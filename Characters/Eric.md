# Eric

## Basic Information

* Birth Name: Eric
* Military Alias: Vane
* Age: 20
* Occupation: Infantry Soldier

---

## Background

Eric disappeared from his native world at age six and spent fourteen years living a completely normal life in the modern world.

At age twenty, he was forcibly returned to his original world.

Without legal status or documentation, he enlisted in the Royal Army under a false identity.

---

## Personality

Eric is practical, cautious, and survival-oriented.

His greatest fear is death.

He does not seek heroism.

He does not seek glory.

He does not seek power.

Despite this, he consistently helps others whenever his conscience refuses to let him walk away.

This creates a constant internal conflict between self-preservation and compassion.

---

## Internal Conflict

Modern Eric believes:

* Killing is wrong
* Human life matters
* Violence should be avoided

Fantasy-world reality teaches:

* Killing is necessary
* Monsters must be fought
* Survival often requires violence

Much of Eric's character journey revolves around reconciling these opposing beliefs.

---

## Long-Term Character Arc

Eric begins as a lost man trying to survive.

Over time, he discovers:

* Who he is
* What he values
* Where he belongs

His ultimate goal is not greatness.

It is building a life, a family, and a future he can call his own.

---

## Hidden Ability

### Dimensional Pocket Space

A tiny dimensional fracture exists within Eric's soul.

Functions include:

* Storage
* Preservation
* Concealment

In extreme situations, the ability can also sever objects through spatial collapse.

Because the ability is forbidden and dangerous, Eric keeps it secret.

For much of the story, he does not fully understand what the ability actually is.

---

Effect:

instantaneous spatial discontinuity of target object
