# Kaelian (Kael)

## Basic Information

- Age: Mid-40s
- Role: Village hunter
- Residence: Eastwood
- Status: Alive

## Appearance
- Big man, broad shoulders
- Quiet face
- Moves like someone who knows how to be still

## Personality
- Speaks rarely
- Observes constantly
- Lets silence do the work
- Deep care buried under layers of quiet
- Not cold — just someone who learned that words are expensive

## Family
- **Wife** — died in childbirth
- **Eldest son** — died in the forest ten years ago
- **Daughter: Lena** — age 16

## Relationship with Eric
- Found Eric naked in the forest; insisted on taking him in
- Never explained why — the village elder later told Eric that Kael didn't bend once he decided
- Gave Eric his old boots (too big, stuffed with rags)
- Shared his last hunter's advice before Eric left
  - *"Don't be the first to volunteer. Don't be the last to fall back."*
  - *"Choose alive."*
- Said "come back" so quietly Eric almost missed it

## Notes
- Stands by the woodpile most mornings, looking at the treeline
- Treats gestures as language — a pelt tossed, a grunt, a nod
- Sees Eric as a shadow of his dead son, whether he admits it or not
