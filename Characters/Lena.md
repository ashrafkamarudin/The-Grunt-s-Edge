# Lena

## Basic Information

- Age: 16
- Role: Hunter's daughter, caretaker
- Residence: Eastwood

## Appearance
- Brown hair tied back
- Plain dress, practical apron
- Quick hands, quicker mouth

## Personality
- Talkative, warm, sharp
- Uses chatter to fill silence
- Protective of people she cares about
- Not afraid to yell at grown men twice her age

## Family
- **Father: Kaelian** — village hunter
- **Mother** — died in childbirth
- **Older brother** — died in the forest ten years ago

## Relationship with Eric
- Took care of him while he recovered
- Talked constantly — told him about goats, neighbours, the blacksmith's son
- First person he spoke to in this world
- Gave him her brother's knife before he left
- Considered him family, called him her brother

## Notes
- Threw a rag at Eric's head when he joked about her cooking
- Told the village he didn't remember where he came from (before he ever spoke)
